=== PART 2: STRATEGY OPTIMISATION ===

Add an edge quality scoring system to value detection:

1. OPPORTUNITY SCORING (0-100):
   Score each potential bet on:
   - Edge size: (edge / 0.15) * 25, capped at 25 points
   - Model confidence: abs(model_prob - 0.5) * 50, capped at 25 points  
   - Historical segment ROI: if this league/market combo has positive historical ROI, add up to 20 points scaled by ROI %
   - Sample reliability: min(segment_bet_count / 20, 1) * 15 points
   - Odds sweet spot: if odds between 1.8-3.5 add 15 points, 1.5-1.8 or 3.5-5.0 add 8 points, outside add 0
   Only place bets where opportunity_score > 60

2. DYNAMIC STAKE SIZING:
   Scale Kelly fraction by opportunity score:
   - Score 60-70: 1/8 Kelly (conservative, small stake)
   - Score 70-80: 1/4 Kelly (standard)
   - Score 80-90: 3/8 Kelly (confident)
   - Score 90+: 1/2 Kelly (high confidence)
   Max stake still capped at bankroll * max_stake_pct (2%)

3. COLD MARKET FILTER:
   If 10+ bets in a league/market combo and ROI < -10%, auto-exclude for 14 days.
   Log as narrative: "Excluding [league] [market] after [N] bets at [ROI]%. Re-evaluating in 2 weeks."

4. HOT STREAK DETECTOR:
   If a combo has positive ROI for 3+ consecutive weeks with 5+ bets/week, add 15 bonus points to opportunity score.
   Log as narrative: "Hot streak: [league] [market] profitable for [N] weeks running."

5. Add to agent_config: min_opportunity_score=60, cold_market_threshold=-10, cold_market_min_bets=10, cold_market_cooldown_days=14, hot_streak_weeks=3, hot_streak_bonus=15

6. Add opportunity_score to paper_bets table and display in dashboard bet history and detail views.
