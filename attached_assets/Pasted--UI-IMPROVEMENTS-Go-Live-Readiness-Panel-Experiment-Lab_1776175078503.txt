# UI IMPROVEMENTS: Go-Live Readiness Panel + Experiment Lab Enhancements

## Part 1: Replace Demo Day Countdown with Go-Live Readiness Panel (PROD ONLY)

The "Journey to Demo Day" countdown on the prod dashboard is no longer relevant. Replace it with a "Go-Live Readiness" panel that shows automated criteria for switching to real money.

### Go-Live Criteria (all must be met — show as a checklist with green ticks/red crosses):

1. **Promoted Markets ≥ 3** — At least 3 league-market combinations at `promoted` tier
   - Show: current count / 3 required
   - List the promoted market names if any exist

2. **Combined Promoted Sample ≥ 100** — Total settled bets across all promoted markets
   - Show: current count / 100 required

3. **Promoted ROI ≥ 3%** — Overall ROI across all promoted-tier settled bets
   - Show: current ROI% (or "No data" if nothing promoted yet)

4. **Promoted CLV ≥ 1.5%** — Overall CLV across all promoted-tier settled bets
   - Show: current CLV%

5. **Candidate Validation ≥ 7 days** — At least one league-market has been in candidate tier for 7+ days in prod with no demotion triggers
   - Show: days since first candidate promotion (or "No candidates yet")

6. **No Active Demotion Flags** — Zero promoted or candidate markets currently flagged for demotion
   - Show: count of flagged markets (should be 0)

### Visual design:
- Show an overall readiness score as a percentage (weighted: sample size and promoted count are 60% of the score, metrics are 40%)
- When ALL criteria are met, show a prominent green "READY TO GO LIVE" banner with a celebratory visual treatment
- When not ready, show "BUILDING CONFIDENCE" with the checklist showing what's still needed
- Include an estimated time to readiness based on current dev betting rate and promotion pipeline velocity
- DO NOT show this panel on dev — dev keeps its existing overview. This is prod-only.

### Also on the prod dashboard:

## Part 2: Experiment Lab Improvements (DEV ONLY)

### 2a: Fix the readiness score weighting

The current "78% ready" on 3-bet experiments is misleading. The readiness percentage must be weighted to reflect that sample size is the hardest and most important gate:

- **Sample size: 50% weight** (this is almost always the bottleneck)
- **Weeks active: 15% weight** (time-based, can't be rushed)
- **ROI: 10% weight**
- **CLV: 10% weight**  
- **Win rate: 5% weight**
- **P-value: 5% weight**
- **Edge: 5% weight**

So an experiment with 3 bets out of 30 required = 10% on the sample dimension = 5% contribution to overall readiness. It should show roughly 10-15% ready, not 78%.

### 2b: De-emphasise unreliable early metrics

When an experiment has fewer than 15 settled bets:
- Show ROI, win rate, and CLV values in a muted/greyed-out style (e.g. 50% opacity)
- Add a small tooltip or label: "Low sample — metrics will stabilise"
- Do NOT show these experiments as "78% ready" — the metric gates being "met" on 3 bets is statistical noise, not signal

When an experiment has 15+ settled bets:
- Show metrics in full colour
- These numbers are starting to become meaningful

When an experiment has 30+ settled bets:
- Full colour, full confidence display
- Show "Ready for promotion evaluation" if other criteria are also close

### 2c: Add estimated time to promotion

For each experiment, calculate and display:
- Current betting frequency for this league-market (bets per week over the last 14 days)
- Based on that rate, estimated weeks until 30 settled bets
- Display as: "~3 weeks to evaluation" or "~12 days to evaluation"
- If betting frequency is 0 (no bets in last 14 days), show "Stalled — no recent activity"

### 2d: Add sorting and filtering

Add controls at the top of the experiment list:

**Sort by (dropdown):**
- Readiness (highest first) — default
- Sample size (most bets first)
- ROI (highest first) — only meaningful for 15+ bet experiments
- CLV (highest first)
- Estimated time to promotion (soonest first)
- Most recent bet (newest activity first)

**Filter by (toggles/chips):**
- Tier: Experiment / Candidate / Promoted / Demoted / Abandoned
- Minimum bets: 0 / 5+ / 10+ / 20+
- League (dropdown of all leagues with experiments)
- Market type: Match Result / BTTS / Over/Under / Corners / Cards
- Hide stalled (no bets in 14+ days) — toggle, default OFF

**Search:** Free text search box that filters by experiment tag name

### 2e: Add a "Top Prospects" summary section

At the top of the Experiment Lab page, before the full list, add a highlighted section:

**"Closest to Promotion"** — Show the top 5 experiments by readiness score that have at least 10 settled bets. These are the ones actually likely to promote soon. Show:
- Experiment tag
- Bets: X / 30
- Estimated time to promotion
- Key metrics (ROI, CLV, WR)
- A mini progress bar

This gives me an at-a-glance view of what's about to graduate without scrolling through 514 experiments.

### 2f: Add a "Recently Active" mini-section

Below "Closest to Promotion", show the 5 experiments with the most recent bet activity. This tells me where the model is currently finding opportunities.

### 2g: Experiment detail view improvements

When clicking into an individual experiment (the expand/detail view), show:
- Full metrics with confidence intervals where possible
- Boosted vs organic performance comparison (win rate and ROI for opportunity_boosted=true vs false within this experiment)
- Betting frequency chart (bets per week over time)
- Cumulative P&L chart for this experiment
- All promotion audit log entries for this experiment
- Current distance to each individual promotion threshold (the "needs X more" display that already exists)
