   - After upsert, call computeTeamXGRolling() for all teams seen
   
   - Function: computeTeamXGRolling()
   - For each unique team in xg_match_data:
     - Get last 10 completed matches (is_result=1), ordered by match_date DESC
     - Compute for last 5 matches:

         (positive = overperforming, negative = underperforming)
       - xg_momentum: compare xg_diff_5 to xg_diff from matches 6-10 
         (positive = improving, negative = declining)
     - Insert new row into team_xg_rolling with current timestamp
   
   - Export function: getTeamXGStats(teamName: string) 
     → returns most recent team_xg_rolling row for that team (fuzzy name match)
