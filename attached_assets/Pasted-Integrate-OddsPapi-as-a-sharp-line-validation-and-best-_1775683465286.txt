1. DAILY FIXTURE MAPPING (1 request/day, run at 6am UTC):

2. ODDS VALIDATION (up to 5 requests/day, during trading cycle):
   GET /v4/fixtures/{fixtureId}/odds?apiKey={key}&marketId={marketId}
   
   OddsPapi market ID mapping (store as a constant):
   - 101 = Full Time Result (1X2) — maps to our MATCH_ODDS
   - 102 = Over/Under Goals — maps to our OVER_UNDER_25 (look for the 2.5 line in response)
   - 103 = Both Teams To Score — maps to our BTTS
   - 104 = Asian Handicap — maps to our ASIAN_HANDICAP
   - 112 = Total Booking Points / Cards — maps to our CARDS
   - 113 = Total Corners — maps to our CORNERS
   
   If a market ID doesn't exist in the response, that market isn't available from OddsPapi for this fixture — fall back to API-Football odds only.

   From each response, extract:
   
   a) PINNACLE LINE:
      Look for bookmaker slug "pinnacle" in bookmakerOdds
      Extract odds for the specific selection we're evaluating
      Convert to implied probability: pinnacle_implied = 1/pinnacle_odds
      Store: pinnacle_odds, pinnacle_implied in odds_snapshots with source "oddspapi_pinnacle"
   
   b) BEST AVAILABLE ODDS:
      Scan ALL bookmakers in response, find highest odds for our selection
      Store: best_odds, best_bookmaker_slug, best_bookmaker_name
      Calculate odds_uplift_pct = (best_odds - api_football_odds) / api_football_odds × 100
   
   c) SHARP-SOFT SPREAD:
      Average odds from sharp bookmakers: pinnacle, singbet, sbobet (whichever are present)
      Average odds from soft bookmakers: bet365, williamhill, ladbrokes, bwin, unibet (whichever present)
      sharp_soft_spread = (soft_avg_implied - sharp_avg_implied) — positive means soft books are offering more value
   
   d) MARKET CONSENSUS:
      Count bookmakers where our selection's implied probability > 50% (i.e. they consider it likely)
      consensus_pct = count / total_bookmakers × 100

=== ENHANCED OPPORTUNITY SCORING ===

For the top 5 candidates that get OddsPapi validation, calculate an enhanced score:

Enhanced Opportunity Score (0-100):
- Edge size using BEST available odds: (edge / 0.12) × 20, capped at 20
- Model confidence: abs(model_prob - 0.5) × 50, capped at 18
- Pinnacle alignment: 
    If model and Pinnacle agree on direction (same side favoured): +15
    If model and Pinnacle within 3% of each other: +10
    If model disagrees with Pinnacle by 3-8%: +0 (neutral, not penalised)
    If model disagrees with Pinnacle by >8%: -10 AND flag as "contrarian" — still allowed but requires score 75+ to proceed
- Sharp-soft spread: min(sharp_soft_spread × 200, 12) — larger spread = more opportunity, cap 12
- Best-odds uplift: if best_odds > API-Football odds by 5%+, add +8. By 10%+, add +12. Cap 12
- Historical segment ROI: if positive history, up to +13 scaled by ROI%
- Odds sweet spot: 1.9-3.2 = +10, 1.5-1.9 or 3.2-4.5 = +5, outside = 0
- Sample reliability: min(segment_bets/20, 1) × 8

Threshold: 65 for Pinnacle-aligned bets. 75 for contrarian bets (going against Pinnacle).
Store enhanced_opportunity_score separately from the preliminary score so we can compare them on the dashboard.

=== DYNAMIC STAKING (revised) ===

For OddsPapi-validated bets, use enhanced score for stake sizing:
- Score 65-72: 1/8 Kelly (cautious)
- Score 72-80: 1/4 Kelly (standard)  
- Score 80-88: 3/8 Kelly (confident)
- Score 88+: 1/2 Kelly (high confidence)
- Contrarian bets (against Pinnacle): multiply Kelly fraction by 0.6 regardless of score — respect the sharp line
- New market types (cards, corners) with <20 settled bets: multiply by 0.7
- Max stake always capped at 2% of bankroll

=== CLOSING LINE VALUE (CLV) — BUDGET-FREE ===

Do NOT use OddsPapi requests for closing line checks. Instead:

1. Record odds_at_placement when bet is placed (already stored)
2. The last API-Football odds fetch before kickoff becomes the closing_odds_proxy (API-Football refreshes every 8 hours — free)
3. Calculate CLV: clv_pct = (odds_at_placement - closing_odds_proxy) / closing_odds_proxy × 100
4. Positive CLV = we got better odds than the market closed at = genuine edge indicator
5. Store clv_pct in paper_bets table, computed during settlement
